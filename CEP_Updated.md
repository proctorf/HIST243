## CEP Memo Reminders (Due May 1)

Each **individual** member must submit their own memo addressing:
1. Intellectual underpinnings of the project and connections to course themes
2. How the project has evolved since the Proposal
3. What you personally have contributed
4. What remains to be done
5. How research has shaped your approach
6. Questions or concerns going forward

## Reflection Paper Reminders

- **Not** a record of what you did — it is an intellectual reflection
- Connect your experience and research to course themes
- Include a self-grade and brief peer grades with justification
- Minimum 750 words; no maximum
- Format: Name, date, and word count (upper left); title centered; double-spaced; 12pt font; Chicago-style footnotes
